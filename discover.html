The Beauty Lies Within

The University of Minnesota Duluth is more than just a campus—it’s a community that truly believes in its students’ potential and their futures beyond graduation. With over 150 majors and minors, UMD offers endless opportunities for exploration, innovation, and growth across every field of study. Ranked as the #4 Regional Public University in the Midwest, UMD is recognized for its academic excellence, dedicated faculty, and hands-on learning experiences that prepare students for real-world success. Beyond the classroom, UMD is home to strong athletic programs and spirited Bulldog pride that bring the campus together. Whether you’re studying by the shores of Lake Superior or cheering from the stands, UMD provides an environment where students are supported, challenged, and inspired to achieve their goals.
